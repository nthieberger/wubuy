5.3 Dual /-w

Dual kin terms are of the type 'his two Fa-s', as opposed to Dyadic forms of the type 'Fa-and-Ch pair'.

The regular Du suffix for nouns is /-w2a:/ ยง4.15), and this is used with VOC/PROP1 and PROP2 kin terms:

/na-baba-wa:/ 'my/our two Fa-s'

/na-ni-nyara-wa:/ 'your two Fa-s'

However, a special suffix /-w2i:yung/ is used in PROP3 forms (i.e., following suffix /-yung/ or /-nyung/):

/na-ni-nyara-yum-bi:yung/ 'his/her/their two Fa-s' These exx. show M NCinfl prefix /na-/.
